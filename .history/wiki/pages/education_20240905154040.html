Science Fair Educational Event:

Kang Chiao International school has two departments, IP department and SP. IP department if for international students that plans to study abroad for university. On the other hand, SP department is for students who are planning to attend local universities in Taiwan. For that reason, some students in the SP department has less awareness about international problems and goals. Therefore, we hosted a session at the conference hall to inform all SP 7th grade students of our project and the problem we intend to solve. We focused on SDGs, micrplastic hazards and a brief introduction to synthetic biology. By doing so, these 7th grader students can get a glimps of international goals and how synthetic biology can impact real life problems.
